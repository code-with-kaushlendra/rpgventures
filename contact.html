Our Legacy

Aviyana HR Solutions, a division of Aviyana Ventures Pvt. Ltd., began its journey as RPG Ventures in 2013 as a Proprietary Firm and transitioned to a Private Limited company in December 2015.

Founded by Dr. Ravindra Pratap Gupta, RPG Ventures was established with a clear vision to deliver expert Human Resource Consultancy, Recruitment, and Staffing Solutions across various sectors including Healthcare, Retail, IT, and ITES.

Guided by our philosophy of "Powered by passion for excellence", we have proudly partnered with some of the leading brands in India and abroad. We specialize in both selective and bulk hiring, and continue to expand our footprint across multiple industries.
