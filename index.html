RPG Ventures HR Solutions - History

Our Working Methodology

RPG Ventures Working Methodology - Recruitment/Talent Acquisition/HR Support Process

We treat our clients as business partners

Once an MOU is signed as per our company & industry norms in mail or written documents the work is initiated without delay.

We try to understand the sectors in which we operate assisted by a team of experienced HR-Consultants on full time role's & Associates with organization with focus on understanding the partners requirement.

A complete manpower mapping with requirement timelines is worked out in association, with the partner and team of knowledgeable dedicated team.

For support structure as well complete requirement is understood and customized services and rates mutually discussed and agreed.

Meeting timely deadlines with precision on requirement specifics is our commitment to our partner companies we associate.

Team shall be working on agreed timelines for various positions/tasks.

A dedicated follow-up shall be ensured at both ends to complete the recruitment process as per agreed timeline.

Besides all-round the year support for issues to be handled in business and life to make it effective and fulfilling personnel and professional journey.
