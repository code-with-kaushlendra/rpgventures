Our Services

Comprehensive HR solutions for your business needs

Domestic Recruitment

We provide tailored solutions to build your business with a differential approach to human capital building. Our team of experts in human resource management ensures the right people are selected for your organization.

Whether your company is hiring managers, executives, or front desk employees, we bring extensive experience to find you the best talent.

We work through headhunting, database systems, job portals, and campus drives to source candidates.

Recruitment Coverage

  • Junior Management
  • Mid Management
  • Top Management

Financial Terms

  • The consultancy pay-out per candidate can be discussed and finalized as per client’s considerations.
  • Calculations are based on CTC, with replacement offered within 45–90 days as required.
  • Payment must be made within 15–30 days from the candidate’s joining date.
  • With confirmation of joining, the client’s HR must share salary break-up details (appointment letter) via email.
  • Invoices are submitted within a week of the candidate’s joining.

Specialized Staffing

  • Back office and support service manpower requirements.
  • Healthcare staffing: Doctors, Nurses, and Hospital Technical Staff.
  • Finance & Legal professionals: Chartered Accountants (CA), Company Secretaries (CS), and Corporate Consultants (part-time & full-time).

International

We work with some of the best international recruiters licensed by Government in their respective countries to ensure that candidates do not face fraudulent contract jobs or are not victimised. We help agencies working in coordination with us, to hire skilled, unskilled and semiskilled employees in various countries from time to time. We also guide candidates on immigration, visa and living conditions with their expected package and lifestyle for various job destinations abroad.We also guide with other health and general regulations criteria.

HR Outsourcing

For companies who wish to outsource HR and get professionals through us, we provide a credible yet affordable alternative solutions to your company and help you focus on your business. We do it with planning, inducting, training, talent management and personnel management.

Payroll Processing

We ensure labour laws and compliances while functioning. This process is done in a clear and flawless way at a miniscule cost for small to big organisations.

Temp / Contract Staffing

As organisations grow, they need people in large number and with compliances, this function is complicating. We understand the need of such growing organisations and provide with such temp/contract staff.
